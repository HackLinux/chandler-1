About MrMenus

MrMenus is a sample parcel that shows programmers how your parcel's own menus and menu items can be incorporated into Chandler's menu structure. It consists of a very simple user interface that includes a parcel menu, and custom menu items inserted in Chandler menus. MrMenus is only a demonstration for programmers designed to go along with a MrMenus tutorial -- it doesn't provide any actual functionality for users.
